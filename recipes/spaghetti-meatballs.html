Spaghetti & Meatballs

Description

Classic spaghetti with tender meatballs in a simple tomato sauce. Comfort food, no drama.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Ground beef (or beef/pork mix)
  • Bread crumbs, egg, parmesan
  • Garlic, onion
  • Tomato sauce / crushed tomatoes
  • Salt, pepper, herbs (basil/oregano)

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted water.
  2. Mix meat, bread crumbs, egg, parmesan, salt, pepper; form meatballs.
  3. Brown meatballs, then simmer in tomato sauce with garlic/onion.
  4. Toss spaghetti with sauce; serve with meatballs and extra parmesan.
