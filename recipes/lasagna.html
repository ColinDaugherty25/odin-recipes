Lasagna

Description

This homemade lasagna is rich, cheesy, and packed with flavor. Perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic, onion, salt, pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Brown the ground beef with garlic and onion.
  4. Layer sauce, noodles, ricotta, mozzarella, and beef in a baking dish.
  5. Repeat layers and top with mozzarella and parmesan.
  6. Bake for 45 minutes, then rest for 10 minutes before serving.
