Chicken Parmesan

Description

Golden, crispy chicken cutlets, marinara, and melty mozzarella. Weeknight hero energy.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts (thin or butterflied)
  • Flour, eggs, bread crumbs
  • Marinara sauce
  • Mozzarella and parmesan
  • Olive oil, salt, pepper

Steps

  1. Bread chicken: flour → egg → bread crumbs.
  2. Pan-fry until golden and cooked through.
  3. Top with marinara and cheeses; broil/bake until melted.
  4. Serve over pasta or with a side salad. Victory lap optional.
